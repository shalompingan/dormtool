🧑‍💻 About Us

We are a team of data-driven creators dedicated to removing the friction from college move-ins and independent living. Our personal experience — wrestling with forgotten dorm essentials, splitting utility bills with roommates, calculating rent on a student budget, and navigating the chaos of moving out — inspired us to build the tools we wish we had.

Every micro-utility on dormtool.com was built with a single philosophy: one problem, one page, zero fluff. No signups, no data collection, no accounts. You open the page, you get the answer, you move on with your day.

Our mission is to equip students and young adults with smart, free, private tools that turn life’s most stressful transitions into manageable checklists. Whether you are packing for your first dorm room, calculating whether you can afford that off-campus apartment, or coordinating a move-out with three roommates — we have your back.

Behind the code, we are builders who believe the web should still be lightweight, fast, and accessible to everyone. Every tool on this site is a single HTML file — no frameworks, no databases, no bloat. Just clean, vanilla JavaScript and CSS that works on any device, anywhere in the world.

Thanks for stopping by. Now go check something off your list.

— The SettleList Labs Team
